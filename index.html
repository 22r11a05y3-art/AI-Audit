What Is an AI Audit?

Your roadmap to smart automation and scalable growth

AI Audit analyzes your business and shows where AI can save time and improve efficiency. We identify repetitive work, automation opportunities, and growth areas so you can act with confidence.

Phase 1 (0-30 Days)

AI Audit and Opportunity Mapping

Objective: Identify where AI creates immediate business impact across revenue, cost, and speed.

1. Executive Alignment (Week 1)

Define business outcomes, not tech goals:

  • Revenue up
  • Cost down
  • Cycle time down
  • Identify top 3 transformation outcomes

2. AI Readiness Assessment

Evaluate current readiness across:

  • Data maturity (structured and unstructured availability)
  • Process digitization level
  • Tech stack (CRM, ERP, APIs)
  • Talent capability

Output: AI Maturity Scorecard

3. Process Mining and Opportunity Identification

Map critical workflows:

  • Sales funnel
  • Customer support
  • Operations and supply chain
  • Finance and reporting

Identify repetitive manual work, decision bottlenecks, and data-rich processes.

Output: Top 10 AI Use Cases

4. Use Case Prioritization Matrix

Rank by business impact, ease of implementation, and data availability.

  • Quick wins (0-30 day ROI)
  • Strategic plays (high leverage opportunities)
